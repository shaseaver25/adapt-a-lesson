# Example: Differentiated Lesson Package

## Lesson: The Water Cycle - Grade 4 Science

---

### Student Group: Reading Level 3-4 (On Grade Level)

**Learning Target:** I can explain the steps of the water cycle and describe how water moves through our environment.

**Key Vocabulary:**
- Evaporation - When water turns into gas (water vapor)
- Condensation - When water vapor turns back into liquid water
- Precipitation - Water falling from clouds (rain, snow, sleet)
- Collection - Water gathering in lakes, rivers, and oceans

**Activity:** Create a diagram showing the water cycle in your neighborhood. Label each step and explain what happens at each stage.

---

### Student Group: Reading Level 1-2 (Below Grade Level)

**Learning Target:** I can name the steps of the water cycle.

**Key Vocabulary (with picture supports):**
- Evaporation ☀️💧➡️💨 - Water goes up into the sky
- Condensation ☁️ - Water makes clouds
- Rain 🌧️ - Water falls down
- Collection 🌊 - Water goes into lakes and rivers

**Activity:** Use the provided water cycle template. Cut and paste the labels in the correct places. Draw arrows to show which way the water moves.

---

### Student Group: Spanish Speakers (ELL)

**Objetivo de aprendizaje / Learning Target:** 
Puedo explicar los pasos del ciclo del agua. / I can explain the steps of the water cycle.

**Vocabulario bilingüe / Bilingual Vocabulary:**
- Evaporación / Evaporation - Cuando el agua se convierte en gas
- Condensación / Condensation - Cuando el vapor de agua se convierte en líquido
- Precipitación / Precipitation - Agua que cae de las nubes
- Colección / Collection - Agua que se junta en lagos y ríos
