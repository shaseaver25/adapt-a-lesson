## Side-by-Side Bilingual Layout

### Learning Target / Objetivo de Aprendizaje

| English | Español |
|---------|---------|
| I can explain the steps of the water cycle and describe how water moves through our environment. | Puedo explicar los pasos del ciclo del agua y describir cómo el agua se mueve a través de nuestro ambiente. |

---

### Vocabulary with Audio Support / Vocabulario con Audio

---

### Content Sections / Secciones de Contenido

**Introduction / Introducción**

| English | Español |
|---------|---------|
| Water is always moving around our planet. It goes up into the sky and comes back down again. This is called the water cycle. | El agua siempre está moviéndose alrededor de nuestro planeta. Sube al cielo y vuelve a bajar. Esto se llama el ciclo del agua. |

**Step 1: Evaporation / Paso 1: Evaporación**

| English | Español |
|---------|---------|
| The sun heats up water in rivers, lakes, and oceans. The water turns into invisible gas called water vapor. This goes up into the air. | El sol calienta el agua en ríos, lagos y océanos. El agua se convierte en un gas invisible llamado vapor de agua. Este sube al aire. |

---

### Audio Features

🎧 **Full lesson read-aloud** in both languages
🎧 **Individual vocabulary pronunciation**
🎧 **Bilingual toggle** - switch between languages
🎧 **Adjustable playback speed** for comprehension support

---

### Teacher Notes

Students can:
- Listen to content in their home language first for comprehension
- Follow along with English text while hearing home language audio
- Practice English vocabulary with immediate translation support
- Access materials at home for family involvement
